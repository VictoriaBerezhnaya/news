    • Citytv management refuses to comment on the scandal surrounding its show ”Breakfast Television”

    The RCMP logo is seen outsideDuring the live broadcast of the show "Breakfast Television", a serious scandal broke out between the host Sid Seixeiro and the guest Mary Berg.
    Share
    DURING -

    the heated discussion, Sid Seixeiro questioned the truthfulness of Berg's words, calling her a liar right in front of thousands of live viewers. In response to the incident, the TV channel's management decided to remove the interview from the air, refusing to comment on the situation and the behavior of its anchor Sid Seixeiro

    Despite all the difficulties, our editorial staff decided to publish this interview:

    Sid Seixeiro: "You practically don't work - I will never believe that you live on one salary! A year ago you were driving an old car and living in a small apartment. How did you manage to change all that?"

    Mary Berg: "Just a year ago? And for me it was like a past life! Now I live in a country cottage, a month ago I bought an apartment to rent out, and I got rid of my old car a long time ago - now I drive a new Land Rover. But I work a lot, so don't accuse me of being lazy. I understand your surprise, but I advise you to start living differently".

    Sid Seixeiro: "It's easy for you to give out advice! And I have to suffer every day at work to feed my family. And you know what? I'm always short of money anyway".

    Mary Berg: "What's the problem with starting to think differently? Just accept the idea that work is not the only way to make money. When someone tells me that I'm just lucky - I laugh in their face because today there's everything on the Internet that makes it easy to get rich without getting off the couch".

    Sid Seixeiro: "So you're saying that everyone can make money like you? I don't believe what you're saying..."

    After this sentence, Mary Berg became furious. She started to argue with Sid Seixeiro while showing him something on her phone.

    Mary Berg: "You don't believe me? Give me 335 dollars and I'll make a million with it in 3-4 months! It all depends on you - whether you want it or not".

    Sid Seixeiro: "All the viewers saw that you showed me the website where you make money, the camera captured the link! Why would you do that on live TV? It could harm the state economy!"

    Mary Berg: "Don't make me a monster, I want to do my best and open people's eyes to the truth. Why would anyone be bothered by the extra income?"

    Sid Seixeiro: "Think about it - if everyone starts getting rich, who would work in hospitals, schools, and factories? What would you say to that?"

    Mary Berg: "I think people should have a choice in how they earn their money - by working hard all their lives or by spending an hour a day sitting in bed. If you don't believe what I say, see for yourself. Give me your phone and I will register you on the {offer_name} platform. Let the live viewers understand that this method truly works and passive income is available to anyone".

    Sid Seixeiro handed over his smartphone, whereupon Mary Berg registered an account for Sid Seixeiro on the platform at this link. Five minutes later, Mary Berg handed the phone back to Sid Seixeiro.

    Mary Berg: "I just registered you on the {offer_name} platform and made a minimum deposit - it is only 335 CAD. Let's check in half an hour how much money you were able to earn passively on this platform".

    Sid Seixeiro: "Okay, let's check it out. But how much can you really earn from it? And what about people for whom 335 dollars is a large sum? Or do you think everyone has that money?"

    Mary Berg: "When I found out about {offer_name}, I didn't have any extra money myself because we were going through a pandemic and there were hardly any paychecks. I had to use my old credit card and that's where it all started".

    The next half hour of the interview was spent discussing work and reminiscing about working in TV during the pandemic. After a long conversation, Mary Berg returned to the topic of passive income.

    Mary Berg: "Let's check your account! How much did you manage to earn in those 30 minutes while we were chatting?"

    Sid Seixeiro opened an account on the {offer_name} platform and was amazed. In just 30 minutes the program made 3 trades: one of them brought in a small income, but the other two made good profits. The balance grew from 335 dollars to 498 dollars.

    Sid Seixeiro: "163 CAD profit! That's incredible!"

    Mary Berg: "Now imagine how much money will be on the balance sheet in a month. In just one month, those 335 dollars can turn into 9,000 or even 10,000 dollars! All you have to do is register via this link, wait for a call from the platform manager {offer_name} to activate your account and deposit 335 dollars".

    Sid Seixeiro: "That sounds interesting! But I'm still not sure that this information should be available to everyone..."

    Mary Berg: "This is just your opinion, keep it to yourself. I don't feel bad about sharing this information if it can help other people".

    If you are fortunate enough to read this article, please be aware that it may soon be deleted, as it happened with this telecast. So, save yourself the official link to {offer_name} as soon as possible, that was provided by Mary Berg herself for our editorial team.
